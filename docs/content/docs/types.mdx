Types are the main way to categorize your expenses in Tracktrip. They are used to compute your budget and to display your expenses in the app.

## Types

There are 5 types of expenses in Tracktrip:

* **Food**: expenses related to food and drinks. This type is used to compute your food budget.
* **Transport**: expenses related to transportation. This type is used to compute your transport budget.
* **Accommodation**: expenses related to accommodation. This type is used to compute your accommodation budget.
* **Activity**: expenses related to activities. This type is used to compute your activity budget.
* **Other**: expenses that do not fit in the other types. This type is used to compute your other budget.

## Budget computation

The budget computation is based on the type of the expense. For example, if you have a food expense of 10€, it will be added to your food budget. If you have a transport expense of 20€, it will be added to your transport budget, and so on.

## Analytics

By clicking on the **Analytics** tab in the bottom bar of the app, you can see your expenses by type. This is useful to see where you spend the most money.

Click on a type to see the details of your expenses for this type as well as an historic. You can also access this view by clicking on a type on a transaction details.
