Tracktrip is a mobile and web application to record and budget your travel expenses, solo or with your friends.

## Create your first travel

[Sign up](http://tracktrip.app/signup) or [login](http://tracktrip.app/login) to access the Tracktrip application.
Click on the **New travel** button or the *+* button in the top right corner.

---

### 1. Fill in travel details
- **Name your trip**: Give your adventure a name (e.g., "Europe by train").
- **Set your dates**: Choose your **start date** and **end date**.
- **Select your currency**: Pick the currency for your travel budget (e.g., EUR).

<Callout title="Note">
You can always edit your travel details later if your plans change.
</Callout>

---

### 2. Set your budget

Define your **daily budget** for different expense types:
- Food
- Accommodation
- Transport
- Activities
- Other

Adjust the amounts as needed. Tracktrip will automatically calculate your **total travel forecast**.
This is useful and recommended to plan how much you will spend during your travel.

<Callout title="Note">
You will be able to adjust those later and during your trip.
</Callout>

### 3. Invite travellers (Optional)
- Invite other travellers to join your adventure.
- Tap **"Invite someone"** to send an invitation via:
  - QR code
  - Shareable link
  - Email address
- You can also skip this step and continue alone.

<Callout title="Note">
You will be able to invite travellers later if needed.
</Callout>

<Card href="/travellers" title="Learn more about categories">
  Categies are used to categorize your expenses.
</Card>

---

### 4. Start your travel

Once you've set up your travel, you're ready to go! Tracktrip will help you manage your expenses and stay on budget.
