Places are a way to track where you spend your money. This is useful if your travel is in multiple places and you want to know where you spend the most money.

## Manage places

### Create places

When creating a transaction, you will be able to select a place. If the place does not exist, you can create it by typing the name of the place and selecting the **Create** option.

### Delete a place

You can manage your places in the **Analytics** tab. Click on one place to see the details. You can then delete the place by clicking on the **Delete** button.

## Analytics

You can see the places where you spend the most money in the **Analytics** tab. You can sort the places by the amount of money spent or by the most recent places.

### Place details

When clicking on a place, you will see the details of the place. You can see the amount of money spent by day and the transactions in this place. Dates are automatically computed based on the transactions in the place.
