You can manage a travel with multiple people. You can add, edit and delete people. You can also see the balance of each person.

## How to add a traveller

You can add a person by clicking on the travel icon in the top left corner, and the clicking on the **Travellers** page. Click on the **Invite** button. You can add a person with multiple way, via link sharing or email directly.

## How to delete a traveller

You can delete a person in the **Travellers** page. Click on the **Delete** button in the menu on the right and confirm the deletion.

## How to see the balance of a person

You can see the balance of a person in the **Balance** tab accessible at the bottom. The balance is the amount of money that a person has to pay or to receive.
