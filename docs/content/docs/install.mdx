Tracktrip is a Progressive Web App (PWA), so you can install it on your Android, iOS, or desktop device easily.

<Callout title="Warning" type="warn">Tracktrip is not yet a native application that you can find on the Play Store or Apple Store. We are working actively on this.</Callout>

---

## **On Android**
1. Open **Chrome** and go to [Tracktrip](https://tracktrip.app/travels).
2. Tap the **three dots (‚ãÆ)** in the top-right corner.
3. Select **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**.
4. Confirm by tapping **Install**.
5. Tracktrip will appear on your home screen and in your app drawer.

---

## **On iOS (iPhone/iPad)**
1. Open **Safari** and visit [Tracktrip](https://tracktrip.app/travels).
2. Tap the **Share button (‚Üë)** at the bottom.
3. Scroll down and choose **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add** in the top-right corner.
5. Tracktrip will be added to your home screen.

---

## **On Desktop (Windows/Mac/Linux)**
